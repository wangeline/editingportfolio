My Video Editing Showcase


During the Covid-19 lockdown, I began learning how to video edit. I learned the basics of editing in the Video Star mobile app. After a few months, I downloaded the Adobe After Effects Program and began learning, using Youtube as my main teaching source. I began an instagram account dedicated to my editing creations on November 23, 2020. Since then, I have continued to create edits dedicated to social media influencers.
